About Us

Bio Veda Action Research company formerly known as Biotique Pvt Ltd was founded by one of the most influential and admired entrepreneurs Ms. Vinita jain in the year 1992. Being a pioneer in natural skin care category, the company has proven track record of providing high quality products.

In India Biotique products are available through 40,000 + multi brands outlets and 1000+ shop in shops. The company has its store in New Delhi. The Company also supplies its products to 300 hotel chains in India.
Support to the Franchisee: Bioveda Action Research Company will continuously assist franchisees in monitoring and managing the business to run it on profit as well as to have satisfied customers.
Set up: The franchisor will assist franchisees in selection of best location for the store. It will also assist in layout, design, architecture, besides purchasing of furniture, equipments and fit-outs.
Training: The franchisor will provide comprehensive training to all franchisees on service operations, standard check up procedures, accounting, inventory control etc. In addition employees from all verticals would be trained to continuously groom and enhance their operating efficiences.
Inventory management: The franchisor will play a major role in stock supply, control checks on goods delivery , replenishment ordering and product range maintenance.
Advertising and promotions: The franchisor in association with franchisee will organise events specific to marketing /branding at the national level including participation in expos, sponsoring events, tie ups with publications, besides promoting franchisees through own website & social media and much more
