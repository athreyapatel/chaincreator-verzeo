BUSINESS DETAILS

About Us
Reliable, Experienced, Trusted pharma product provider

Sanjivani is a well known name in the pharmacy segment of the country providing comprehensive, world class solutions in the field of pharmaceutical retail and distribution. Sanjivani specializes in providing a complete bouquet of products under its store, particularly in the pharmaceutical industry. The brand, backed by NB Marketing Pvt. Ltd. as its parent company specializes in providing the following products through its world class outlets, in and around Delhi: Allopathic medicines, Ayurvedic medicines, OTC products, SUrgical products and general daily use products.
The company is a reputed name in the field of distribution and aims to provide people complete solutions catering largely to their health care needs.
Largest Item Master: The brand is one of the largest item master in the country, specializing in fulfilling almost any prescription that comes to its outlet.
Technology driven service provider: The brand is known to monitor sales, stock turnover and inventory shelf life through advanced software to minimize dead stock and manage on-time return and inter-store transfers.
Daily stock replenishment: The brand, in an endeavor to provide quick and quality services, believes in daily ordering and replacement system of stock management which ensure in-a-day availability of out of stock items, enhancing the experience for end user.
