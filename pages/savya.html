About Us
- N. K. Chains Private Limited has earned a huge respect as an accomplished exporter, supplier, distributor and manufacturer of quality driven Jewellery Items. We stepped into the industry with manufacturing of gold chains in 1999. After getting great success and client satisfaction, we came up with our brand name called SAVYA JEWELS, in 2010.
- Savya Jewels, is an emerging brand in the Indian and foreign market for its Italian jewellery, offering a wide range of diamond jewellery like Diamond Pendants, Diamond Necklace etc. to give an elegant touch to your style. The diamonds used in the crafting of Pendants, Rings, Earrings and other products are procured from few of the leading and highly trusted dealers around the world. Our products are constantly marketed in India and Aboard, therefore, people are appreciating the new concept in the jewellery which is lighter in weight.
- We are looking to make distibutors both in Indian and foreign market. In year 2016, we entered into Italian Jewellery manufacturing with the State of Art machinery which came for the first time in India. .
