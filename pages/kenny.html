About Us
- The first Kenny Rogers restaurant was opened in coral spring, Florida in August of 1991.
- Till today, the company continued to emphasize freshly made products and its rotisserie roasted chicken as family and individual meals.
- Internationally, Kenny Rogers ROASTERS has expanded to various countries around the world and has restaurants in Malaysia, Singapore, China, Indonesia, Brunei, Philipines and very soon in Qatar.
- The Kenny Rogers ROASTERS' roaster concept was first introduced in 1991 by John Y. Brown, Governor of the state of Kentucky who was also one of the original developers of Kentucky Fried Chicken.
- Brown teamed up with his musician friend Kenny Rogers. Both men were drawn to the idea of serving home-style family foods.
