About Us
Rasna – Mocktail Bar is a revolutionary venue that’s so much more than your bar. We have taken alcohol out of the mix to provide a truly welcoming, creative and nostalgic space where people from all background and walks of life can hang out, socialize and relax.
We have built our beverage menu using the 90’s favourite drink “Rasna” and to complement the varied it we have added regional delicacies.
As the name and tag line (take a sip) suggest, Rasna Mocktail Bar is a first-of-its-kind kiosk bar concept specializing in mocktails and other lifestyle coffees. The showmanship of shaking, stirring and flaring of drinks in front of guests making the concept even livelier.
