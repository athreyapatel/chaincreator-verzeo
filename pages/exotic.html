About Us

NOTION - is inviting franchisees to join hands and earn high profits to be a successful entrepreneur...Read On!

NOTION- luxury flooring concept, is the INDIA’S leading brand with clear focus on quality and durable product. It is known for its premium quality and customized solutions for Interior and Exterior Wood Flooring Concepts. NOTION – the flagship brand of NOIDA based EXOTIC DÉCOR PVT. LTD. was established in 2002. After running the successful show for almost 10 years, EDPL launched a premium brand “NOTION”–Luxury Flooring Concepts.

Today the flooring has become such a popular product, that it is widely been used in residences, housing projects, Hotels, Retail showrooms, Office complexes and much more. Now to be the premium brand, EDPL has introduced wide range of products varying from mid to high end concepts for all flooring needs, whether it be interiors or exteriors.

Today the market for wood flooring products is growing at a pace of 30-35% per year.

The brand set to grow high with expansion across INDIA. NOTION is looking for potential master franchise partners in all major states of India and franchisee stores in all tiers 1 & 2 cities in Pan India. NOTION aims to have at least 100 stores in India.
