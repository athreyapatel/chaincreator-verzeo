About Us
- MGreen Sun Planters is a unit of M/S Dee Dee Enterprises. Seeing the current trend and awareness among people related to environment prompted us to introduce the product which can be helpful to the society. Thats how Green Sun Planters was born. We are trying to cater the niche market of plant lovers. We have imported the wonderful product from China known as self watering planters.
